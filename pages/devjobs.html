Our story

We develop high performing online shop software. Established for years, we are one of the market leaders worldwide. More than 140,000 businesses use our online shop solution already.

We love e-commerce and we always have an eye on the latest trends on the market.

What we offer

Working at ePages means being part of a growing international team, full of e-commerce experts who are passionate for web development. In the backend we are working modular with Java, JPA, MySQL, MongoDB and Solr which is decoupled from the Node.js frontend by a REST-API. We also work with GitHub, Vagrant, Jenkins, Selenium, Docker and Jira. We place great emphasis on test-driven-development and continuous integration.
